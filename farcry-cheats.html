    Player Cheats:

  • [Backspace]: godmode on/off
  • [P]: all weapons
  • [O]: 999 ammo for equipped weapon
  • [F1]: change view (first/third person)
  • [F4]: no clip
  • [F2]: next checkpoint
  • [F9]: save location of the player
  • [F10]: jump to saved location
